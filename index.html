Fatal Shark Attacks in Australia

Where in Australia do Shark Attacks Occur?

NSW and QLD had significant spike in attacks in early 1900s. WA experienced a notable increase in recent decade. VIC, TAS and SA show lower and steader rates of attacks over time.
